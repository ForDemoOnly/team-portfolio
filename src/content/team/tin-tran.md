I graduated from Vietnam – Korea University of Information and Communication Technology (VKU). My career started early — in my sophomore year — as a full-stack developer working on e-learning, movie streaming, and carpad projects (FE, BE, DevOps).

After 3 years in web/app development, I found my true inspiration in **C++**. That marked a turning point. I shifted to the **automotive industry**, joining a company building in-vehicle infotainment systems with C++ and Qt. I’ve since developed features for world‑famous car brands: map displays, music players, in‑car games, camera systems, and settings panels.

I’ve never pursued a Team Lead or higher title — my passion has always been **pure coding**.

Today, I’m still working in automotive, but in my free time, I’m chasing another dream: **indie game development**.
